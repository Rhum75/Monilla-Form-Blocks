About Us

Overview Our Company Vision & Mission Partner With Us

Strength for Lifetime Use

Allied Concrete Products, Inc. is a major concrete pipe, manhole, and block manufacturer trusted by the Philippine construction industry. Established in 1960, we have been at the forefront of providing quality concrete products for infrastructure and construction projects across the nation.

Our production plants are strategically located in Metro Manila, Laguna, and Bulacan, ensuring efficient service and prompt delivery to projects throughout the Philippines. With over six decades of experience, we pride ourselves on our technical knowledge, design expertise, and commitment to product quality.

Through continuous investment in updated equipment and flexible production facilities, we remain responsive to the dynamic requirements of our clients and the evolving demands of the construction industry.
