About Us

About Us

Where quality meets innovation, our company is dedicated to producing durable and reliable precast wall form blocks for modern construction. We focus on strong materials, consistent workmanship, and dependable service to support safe and long-lasting building projects.

Overview Our Company Vision & Mission Partner With Us

Strong Walls for Lasting Construction

MCPM is dedicated to producing high-quality precast wall form blocks designed to support strong and durable construction. We are committed to providing reliable building materials that help ensure safe and efficient construction projects.

Our company focuses on consistent product quality, proper design, and dependable service to meet the needs of builders, contractors, and construction professionals. We strive to deliver products that contribute to long-lasting structures and modern building development.

Through continuous improvement and dedication to quality workmanship, MCPM aims to provide dependable construction solutions and support the growing demands of the construction industry.

Our Company

Our History

MCPM was established in 2010 in Dasmariñas Cavite to provide precast wall form blocks for residential and commercial construction.

Our Facilities

Our production facility is located near Mason Temple at 346 Salitran 3, Dasmariñas, Cavite, and is equipped with specialized tools such as dowel straight-cut wire and molded plantsa vibration equipment to ensure efficient and high-quality manufacturing of precast concrete products.

Our Products

We produce precast wall form blocks available in Conventional, V-Cut, and Shiplap designs, designed for strong and durable wall construction.

Quality & Standards

We maintain product quality through regular inspection, careful material selection, and proper production processes to ensure strong and durable construction materials.

Our Projects

Our products are used in residential and small commercial projects such as houses and building walls, supporting safe and stable construction.

Our Locations

MCPM operates in Cavite, serving construction projects in nearby areas with reliable product supply and delivery.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and leading supplier of high-quality precast concrete products in the Philippines, known for reliability, durability, and excellent service.
